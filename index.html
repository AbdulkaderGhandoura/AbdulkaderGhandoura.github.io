Summary

Master's student in Data Engineering and Analytics at the Technical University of Munich with a current research focus on Artificial Intelligence in Medicine. Gaining hands-on experience through an internship at the university hospital, Rechts der Isar. Holds a bachelor's degree in Information Systems Engineering. Passionate about problem-solving, with six years of experience in the International Collegiate Programming Contest as a contestant, coach, problem-setter, and judge. DAAD scholar supporting fellow Syrian students in pursuing postgraduate studies abroad through academic mentorship programs with Syrian Youth Empowerment and Jusoor.
