Summary

DAAD scholar and Data Engineering and Analytics master's student at the Technical University of Munich with a research focus on Artificial Intelligence in Medicine. Currently doing an internship at the Rechts der Isar hospital in Munich as part of the master's study. Holds a bachelor's degree in Information Systems Engineering with significant problem-solving experience through involvement in the International Collegiate Programming Contest, for six years, as a contestant, coach, problem-setter, and judge. Since moving to Germany, has volunteered as a mentor in both Jusoor's Academic Mentorship Program and SYE's Syria Grad Program to assist fellow Syrian students in pursuing postgraduate studies abroad.
